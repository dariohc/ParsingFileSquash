Squash League Rules

Please submit your results in the usual way, as the "best of five" sets. You can also enter incomplete results if you run out of time or a player retires injured, so all of the following results are considered valid:

3-0 3-1 3-2 2-0 2-1 2-2 1-0 1-1 0-0

If you want to claim a "walkover" you will need to email the organizer, with Cc: to your opponent, but be aware that walkovers are only awarded in exceptional circumstances.

Please ignore the Totals column. It shows the total number of sets won, not the total of points awarded for the results.

See   http://w4.estec.esa.int/sscc/squash/mini_league.php   for the complete rules and calculation details.
